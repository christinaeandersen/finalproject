Giving textile waste a second chance

The fast-fashion system has resulted in a major overflow of textile waste. Our mission is to move to a more circular fashion industry, where we reuse textiles instead of wasting them.
We believe that the discarded textiles deserve a second chance since most of the materials are perfectly fit for reuse in new designs. That is why we transform discarded jeans and workwear, into modern and affordable sneakers.
